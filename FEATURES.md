# New Features Added

## 1. RDR2-Style Radial Menu
- **Activation**: Right-click anywhere in the game
- **Features**:
  - 8 quick-access slots
  - Visual highlight on hover
  - Instant tool/building selection
  - Semi-transparent overlay
  - Quick access to: Build, Demolish, Road, House, Restaurant, Shop, Terrain tools

## 2. Progression & Money Earning System
- **Monthly Income System** (every 3 seconds = 1 game month)
  - Restaurants: $200/month
  - Fast Food: $200/month
  - Shops: $150/month
  - Malls: $150/month
  - Petrol Stations: $180/month
  - Offices: $100/month
  - Factories: $120/month
  - Houses: $50/month (tax)
  - Apartments: $100/month (tax)

- **Monthly Expenses**:
  - Police/Fire Stations: $100/month
  - Schools/Hospitals/Libraries: $80/month
  - Town Hall: $150/month
  - Power Plants: $200/month
  - Water Towers: $100/month
  - Roads: $5/month
  - Traffic Lights: $10/month

- **Vehicle Types**:
  - Cars (Blue, Speed 2)
  - Trucks (Gray, Speed 1)
  - Buses (Orange, Speed 1)
  - Taxis (Yellow, Speed 2)
  - Police Cars (Blue, Speed 3)

- **Features**:
  - Vehicles spawn automatically on roads
  - Random vehicle types
  - Smooth movement along roads
  - Vehicles despawn when off-map

## Controls
- **Right-Click**: Open radial menu
- **Left-Click**: Place building/use tool
- **Middle-Click or Ctrl+Left**: Pan camera
- **Q/E**: Rotate camera
- **WASD**: Pan camera

## Progression Flow
1. Start with $50,000
2. Build residential buildings (houses/apartments)
3. Add utilities (power plant, water tower) for resident happiness
4. Build roads for traffic
5. Add commercial buildings (restaurants, shops, petrol stations) for income
6. Watch population grow as residents move in
7. Manage monthly income vs expenses
8. Expand city with services (police, fire, schools, hospitals)
9. Add traffic lights at busy intersections
10. Monitor city happiness and adjust accordingly
